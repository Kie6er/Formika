At all stages, we work closely with the Client to ensure the exact execution of the project, compliance with deadlines, because high quality of execution is our main priority.
We offer a full range of services related to the creation of museum exhibitions, including:
  • Conceptual design: a team of specialists works together with the client to develop a unique concept of the exhibition, taking into account its goals, theme, audience and budget.
  • Concept development: Based on conceptual design, we create a detailed description of the exhibition, including plans, graphics and visualizations.
  • Production and installation of expositions: creation of necessary exhibition elements, suchas stands, displays, models, interactive stations and other objects necessary for the exposition.
  • Technical support: After the completion of the project, we can provide technical supportand maintenance of the exhibition elements to ensure their proper functioning and durability.
